Attribute System

The Attribute System has undergone a complete rework, transitioning from Attribute Spirits to the new Attribute Stones. These powerful stones can be obtained by participating in special raids or by exploring the Chromatic Highlands and Garden of Genesis. Each Attribute directly influences your character's skills and elemental affinity. Players can visit Attribute Master Yin in Aden Town to exchange their collected Attribute Stones and Potions for powerful enhancements.

When an attribute is imbued in a weapon, it increases that attribute's attack power. Conversely, attributes in armor increase resistance against their opposite attributes. For example, armor imbued with the Water attribute provides Fire Resistance.

Weapons can reach a maximum attribute value of 300, while armor pieces are limited to 120. Each armor piece can hold up to 3 different attributes. The first stone inserted into a weapon adds 20 attribute points, while subsequent stones add 5 points each. For armor, both Attribute Stones and Crystals add 6 points to resistance. To increase attribute values beyond 150 for weapons and 60 for armor, Attribute Crystals are required.

For armor pieces, Attribute Stones increase resistance up to 60. Added stones or crystals increase your resistance to the opposite attribute (for example, Earth Stones increase Wind Resistance). For enhancement beyond 60, you must use Attribute Crystals. The maximum resistance achievable for a single attribute in armor is 120.

In combat, Attribute Resistance directly reduces the attacker's Attribute Power by the resistance value. For example, if an attacker has 300 Fire Attribute against a defender with 120 Fire Resistance, the effective attack power would be 180 (300 - 120), roughly a 18% increase in damage (rather than 30%).
