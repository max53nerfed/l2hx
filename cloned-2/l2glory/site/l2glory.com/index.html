Talismans

Talismans are powerful items that grant unique enhancements to your character. Available to all classes, talismans can be equipped for both PvP and PvE encounters. Each talisman provides different effects, depending on its type — offering boosts that can turn the tide of battle. Equip wisely and unleash your character’s full potential.

  • Increase basic stats
  • Increase resistance to negative effects
  • Remove negative effects

    • Simplified purchase of talismans

  • All talismans are sold by GM Shop – Talisman Store Butler for Glory Coins

    • Equipment System

  • Up to 3 bracelet slots available
  • Personal, non-tradeable items
  • Instant passive effect activation

    • Varieties of Talismans

  • Upgrade using Glory Coins and PvP Coins
  • Visual effects at Level 6 and 7
  • Only highest-level talisman stats apply
