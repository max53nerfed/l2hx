Basic Information
All characters appear in starting locations.
All teleports up to level 40 are free.
Global chat is available.
The starting buffer is available up to level 80. (Reduced level of buffs.)
24+4 buff slots available, main buff duration – 20 minutes, dances/songs – 2 minutes. (The drop of books for additional slots is standard.)
No MP potions available.
Offline trade and craft: .offline command (0 Adena), trade zones – Town of Giran.
Commands to disable experience, shot animations, and costumes – via .menu.
Inventory slots increased to 250, overweight is disabled.
/unstuck command – 2 minutes (coldowns time - 5 minutes)
Premium Account in a group is only required for the player who delivers the final hit to mobs.
Server Features
Number of clients on one computer: 1+1. The price of the second window is 2 Coin of Luck for 7 days.
The server does not have premium accounts and runes.
The option to play on 2 clients is implemented: Classic and Interlude
An active buff block button is available when creating a character.
An improved Interface for the Interlude client.
The server has reworked mob damage from physical classes (increased PVE damage).
The Zero Hour quest is standard.
In the rooms of the Imperial Tomb marked on the map, the drop of Mid-Life Stones has been increased.

Weapon Augmentation
The active buff from the Life Stone disappears when the weapon is removed.
Augmented weapons cannot be traded but can be sold through the Market.
The duration of buffs from augmentation skills: 10-20 minutes. Chance-based buffs last 15 seconds (80% chance).
Chance of dropping Top Life Stones from Tyrannosaurus is 10%.
Chance of dropping Top Life Stones from Pterosaur is 1%.
You can enhance augmentation effects with the "Upgrade Augment" skill obtained from the NPC "Augmentation Expert."

Career
First Profession: Quest or purchase with Coin of Luck.
Reward: 10 D-Grade coupons.
Second Profession: Quest or purchase with Coin of Luck.
Reward: 10 C-Grade coupons.
Third Profession: Full quest, no purchase option.
Reward: 5kk Adena + Book of Giants.
Sub-Class: Talk to the chest after killing Shilen’s Messenger Cabrio, character above level 75, to receive Sub-Class Certificate.
Maximum number of Sub-Classes: 5
New Sub-Classes: Overlord and Warsmith.
New Sub-Classes: Elf can add Sub-Class professions of Dark Elf and vice versa.
Noblesse: Flame of Splendor Barakiel, level 75, respawn every 12 hours ±30 minutes.
Noblesse Quest: Standard
Alternative for Barakiel: If you deal at least 1 hit to the raid boss and are within 1000 radius, you will automatically receive a fragment of the Staff of Goddess - Rain Song.
To complete the quest, collect 8 such fragments and exchange them in ALT+B for Caradine's Letter.

Standard clan level increase or purchase for Festival Adena.

Events
Dice Duel
Place a bet from 1 to 20 coins, and challenge other players to a duel. Each participant rolls a dice, and the one with the higher number takes the entire pot.
Bet placement fee: 10k Adena
Team vs Team
For each opponent killed, the team gets one point. The team with the most points wins at the end. Attacking allies or healing/buffing enemies is prohibited.
Maximum number of players: 200
Event times: 10:00 | 18:00 | 23:00 (GMT +2)
Death Match
Welcome to Death Match! In this competition, there are no allies, no refuge - only one goal: survive at all costs. Show your combat skills and adaptability to changing conditions.
Maximum number of players: 100
Event times: 12:00 | 20:00 (GMT +2)
Capture the Flag
Welcome to Capture the Flag! In this exciting competition, your task is to capture the enemy's flag and bring it to your base, while defending your own flag from enemy players.
