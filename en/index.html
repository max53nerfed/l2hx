Our Version of Remastered

The concept of L2 Hex revolves around the idea of taking the best elements from every version of Lineage II and combining them for you. We have drawn from the familiar Interlude, the enduring High Five, and the new changes from Essence, all on a modern, stylized Classic base. We are more than just Classic.

A modern, stylized, and optimized client derived from the latest chronicles of the official game servers.
Nostalgic items and zones from Interlude and High Five, relive the old days in a new world!
A skill-based Olympiad system similar to High Five and Classic, where the agility of your hands matters more than the size of your wallet.
Over 100 new skins and accessories from all chronicles to customize your character and make them feel unique to you.
Reworked buff system, similar to Goddess of Destruction.
Addition of a pet system, similar to Interlude and High Five.

Unseen and brand-new unique content.

Begin with bespoke: our server is uniquely tailored to the experience of each player and their playstyle. Our philosophy is a "leave no stone unturned" approach to the player experience, whether it be the blood rush from intense PvP battles, the rewards of helping local NPCs with their tasks, or the serenity of exploring and enjoying the vast, beautiful environment on the continent of Aden. In L2 Hex, you will not only find new items, skills, and mechanisms but also a completely different approach to the entire game!

Our Version of Classic

Beyond the Glory Days lies the Secrets of Empire, the last universally accepted version of "Classic" for most players. Our vision is to reimagine what "Classic" could have been, rather than what it is now.

We strive to blend nostalgia with innovation, creating a timeless experience that honors the past while embracing the future. Join us on this journey as we explore a new realm of possibilities in L2 Hex!

Reworked Skills and Classes

Tired of playing on the same servers with unbalanced classes and poor design? Look no further! We have the solution for you. L2 Hex x3 offers a wide variety of modified and revamped classes, giving new life and a unique identity to each player. Classes such as Bladedancer, Swordsinger, Spoiler, Maestro, and more have been modified to reinforce their identity and provide a distinct feel. Additionally, Kamaels have been disabled, as we do not believe they fit in line with our vision.

We strongly believe that everyone should have a voice, and no matter which class they choose to play, that voice will always be heard.

Improved Armor Sets, Weapons and More!

Reminisce about your glory days or create new ones with improved, nostalgic armor sets reintroduced on L2 Hex x3.

Unpopular sets such as Tallum, Dark Crystal, Avadon, Doom and Zubei sets have been modified to feature new and impactful effects.

We have also added new items such as Agathions, Talismans from Essence and more. Additionally, we have reworked the existing item system from Classic to ensure balanced gameplay.

Modernized Zones and Instances

Zones across the world of all levels have been reworked with dynamic monster counts and spawn times based on our innovative grid system. This ensures that players have enough monsters to fight when the zone is contested.

Furthermore, we have added new daily and weekly instances for both parties and solo players to allow them to participate in activities fairly. There are also daily instances for Queen Ant, Orfen, Core, Zaken, and Baium to obtain damaged jewels.

Modern, Enchanced Interface

Relax with the game using a unique user interface and refreshed graphics designed by our team! A delightful Community Board and new, readable windows for popular commands will make your time spent in the game a pleasure. You will rest, admiring the true art of elves!

Creature comforts, along with new innovations, are included to provide users with the best possible Lineage II experience. Many features added are crafted as if the official developers themselves had made them.

International Community

L2 Hex is not just a server, but above all a gaming community. We want everyone to feel good here, to make L2 Hex a refuge for people, a place of relaxation and escape from reality. Therefore, we pay special attention to the principles that will help us make this vision a reality.

To further iterate this, an example of the following clans, parties and more will be joining us:
Gravity, OsoToxico, AdrenalineTM, FakeCP, Insanity, Toxic, Pride, BrKillers, SerieA, Tempest, joj, 307, Praetorians, HitMe, pavuk, FranPinHead
and more!

Fixed Prime Time and Stages

Epic PvP battles are the heart of every server, so we've ensured that players from around the world can compete. L2 Hex schedules key battles for Epic Bosses at prime times for North and South America and Europe, ensuring everyone can join the fight! Smaller clans and solo players can also compete for Raid Points and Fame and battle over Clan Strongholds in 'hot zones,' instances, and farming spots. We have designed our gameplay to be comfortable and relaxed, accommodating those with busy lives, so you don't need to be constantly glued to your screen (though you might want to be!).

Sieges
 Details

Sieges will take place every Sunday at 21:00 GMT +2 and GMT -2 (server time).

Epic Bosses
 Details

Queen Ant, Core, Orfen and Zaken have decreased difficulty and rewards due to them being twice a day and a server-wide event.

Elite Epic Bosses
 Details

Baium has decreased difficulty and rewards. due to them being twice a day and a server-wide event.

Mythic Bosses
 Details

Freya and Beleth spawn every Tuesday and Thursday at 22:00 GMT +2 and GMT -2 and will reward participants with Mythic Fragments.

Olympiad
 Details

The Grand Olympiad commences two weeks after from the launch of the server on Friday to Saturday. Only main classes are allowed to be used in the Grand Olympiad and special buffs will be disabled The best player in their class will be given Hero status for a week when distributed on Sunday.
